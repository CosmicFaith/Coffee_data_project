# ComsicFaith.github.io
Analyzing coffee sales from a vending machine in Ukraine. 

# Questions 
- Do repeat customers (same card number) have consistent ordering habits, or do they switch up what they buy? Are "regulars" loyal to one drink, or do they explore the menu?
- What time are people getting what drinks? Is there a clear "morning coffee" pattern vs "afternoon pick-me-up" pattern, and do the drink choices differ between those windows?
- Is there a relationship between cash vs card payments and what people order or how much they spend? Do card users spend more per transaction than cash users?

# Findings
We learned that consumers are usually loyal to one drink when returning. Most drinks are bought from 7:30 am to 11 pm, with peaks at 8 am, 5 pm, and 6 pm. Additionally, the most frequently purchased drink with cash is a latte, whereas the most frequently purchased drink with a card is an americano with milk. And there is a significant difference in the amount spent with the different payment methods, with card being the dominant method.
